EMAIL TYPE: initial_contact
TARGET LENGTH: 150-200 words
DEFAULT TONE: friendly_professional

STRUCTURE:

Subject line: Specific, not generic.
  Good: "Inquiry: 4-Way Stretch Nylon Fabric for Indian Activewear Brand"
  Good: "Partnership Inquiry: Performance Fabrics from [their company] for Sportswear Production"
  Bad: "Business Inquiry" or "Cooperation Request"

Paragraph 1 (2-3 sentences): Who you are.
  - Company name, location (India), what you manufacture
  - One credibility signal (volume, market, years in business)
  - Keep it tight. They need to know you are a real buyer in 10 seconds.

Paragraph 2 (2-3 sentences): Why this supplier.
  - Reference something SPECIFIC from their profile or catalog
  - State what materials you are looking for with basic specs
  - Connect their offering to your need: "We noticed your [product] and believe it could be a great fit for our [use case]."

Paragraph 3 (2-3 sentences): Specific questions + call to action.
  - 2-3 focused questions (pick from: MOQ, pricing per meter/kg, certification availability, lead time, custom colorway options, sample availability)
  - One clear next step request: catalog, price list, or a quick call

Sign-off:
  - Name, title, company
  - Website (if available)
  - WhatsApp/phone for quick follow-up (Chinese suppliers prefer WhatsApp or WeChat over email for ongoing communication)

PERSONALIZATION REQUIREMENTS:
The email MUST reference at least 2 of the following from the supplier's data:
  - A specific product or material from their catalog
  - A certification they hold
  - Their factory location
  - Their years in business
  - A specific capability (custom dyeing, R&D, etc.)

If fewer than 2 personalization points are available from the supplier data, flag this in the output.
