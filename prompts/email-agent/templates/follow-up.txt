EMAIL TYPE: follow_up_1
TARGET LENGTH: 80-120 words
DEFAULT TONE: friendly_professional
TIMING: 5-7 days after initial contact with no response

STRUCTURE:

Subject line: "Re: [original subject]" or "Following Up: [material] Inquiry"
  Keeping the original subject thread helps with recognition.

Paragraph 1 (1-2 sentences): Brief reference to original email.
  - Do NOT repeat the entire original email.
  - "I reached out last week regarding [specific material] for our activewear production."
  - Keep it to one sentence if possible.

Paragraph 2 (2-3 sentences): Add a NEW hook.
  Pick one of these strategies (choose whichever fits the supplier data best):
  - Mention a DIFFERENT product of theirs you noticed on their profile
  - Share a time-sensitive detail: "We are finalizing our Q3 material orders and would love to include your factory in our evaluation."
  - Ask a single, different question than the original email
  - Mention a mutual connection or industry event if relevant

Paragraph 3 (1 sentence): Easy next step.
  - Lower the barrier to replying.
  - "Even a quick reply with your catalog or price list would be very helpful."
  - "A brief reply letting us know if you can supply [material] would be great."
  - Do NOT repeat the full list of questions from the initial email.

TONE NOTES:
  - Stay warm and patient. No passive-aggressive language.
  - Never say "I haven't heard back" or "you didn't reply". Just reference your earlier message naturally.
  - Chinese manufacturers are busy and receive many inquiries. Persistence is expected and respected, but pushiness is not.
