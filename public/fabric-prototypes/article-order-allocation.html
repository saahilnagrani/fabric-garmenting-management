Reading the allocation cell

A row tells you, at a glance, how the demand is being met.

Green: kg drawn from FabricReceipts already in our hands or at the garmenter. Ochre: kg pre-allocated against a FabricOrder not yet received. Hatched: shortfall — demand the system can't yet back from any source. Solid terracotta: an over-allocation, possible when an order intentionally absorbs surplus.

Coverage % on the right is the same data summarized. A row reading 100% green means cutting can start immediately.

Men's shirt · indigo 7-2

  • · from FO-504 (not yet received) 30.0 kg
