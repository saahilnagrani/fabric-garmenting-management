The seven views

click to open
01existing · enhanced
Fabric orders

Orders table with inline expandable receipts timeline. Adds On-order, In our hands, At-garmenter columns. One row over-receives 250/200kg with a surplus banner.
02new
Receive fabric

Sheet for logging a FabricReceipt against an order, then a post-save branch: allocate to articles, or dispatch to a garmenter.
03new
Dispatch to garmenter

Send fabric out of our custody to a specific garmenter as a GarmenterDispatch. After save, prompt to allocate at that garmenter.
04new · headline
Garmenter view

Per-garmenter screen showing every fabric currently with them: dispatched, allocated, reserved, remaining. Replaces the existing Garmenting Plan PDF.
05existing · enhanced
Article orders & allocation

Article orders grid with a stacked allocation cell: from-received + from-expected + shortfall. Mixed-source backing visible at a glance.
06existing · enhanced
Phase planning · both modes

Existing Quantity and Fabric modes are kept; underneath each, a "What this commits" panel shows the Product, FabricOrder and new Allocation rows the click will write.
